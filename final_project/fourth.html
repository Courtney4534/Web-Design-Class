Gardening in Your House

Gardening in your home can be just as rewarding as gardening outside as you are still able to grow many different fruits and veggies without having a whole garden to take care of. There are some problems with gardening in door like, sunlight reduction, forgetting to water, and temperature fluctuation. Plants that need a certain environment to grow may not be the best to grow inside your home, but microgreens and some bush fruits are easy to grow inside your home!

Tips and Tricks

  • Use indoor potting soil
  • Containers with drainage
  • Humidity 40% to 50%
  • Plant near visible windows
  • Find a space with a lot of room for the plants to grow
  • Make a watering schedule
  • Change pot sizes as they grow

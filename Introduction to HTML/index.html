Mushrooms are super interesting and useful for everyday life. Scientists have discovered many new uses for mushrooms that range from a natural source of caffeine to strains that help with sleep. Reishi is a mushroom that supports immune, kidney, liver, cardiovascular, and nervous system functions making it super versatile for everyone to use as a daily supplement. Another type of mushroom that is used in making coffee and helping with focus is Lion's Mane,as it targets the brain and the production of nerve growth factor. I have taken these types of mushrooms before and I can say they help with brain fog, keeping me energized throughout the day, and I retain information way easier. With expanding research of these funguses we could change they way we live by adding these mushrooms into our daily lives.
